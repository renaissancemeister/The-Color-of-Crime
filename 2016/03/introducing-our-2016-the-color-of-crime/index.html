The Color of Crime

Introducing our 2016 ‘The Color of Crime’

Jared Taylor of American Renaissance presents the 2016 edition of the classic report, The Color of Crime. Taylor cites government statistics to show that there is no evidence of systemic bias in policing or in the justice system. The facts leave no doubt that people of different races commit crime at sharply different rates, and that interracial violence is overwhelmingly black-on-white.
